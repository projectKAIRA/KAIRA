How it works

From inbox to action
in seconds.

Step 01

Email arrives

KAIRA monitors your inbox around the clock. The moment an email lands, it gets to work — no delays, no missed messages.

Step 02

AI reads everything

PDFs, Word docs, Excel files, images, OneDrive links — KAIRA reads and understands every format your customers send.

Step 03

Instantly classified

Purchase order, RFQ, or general inquiry — KAIRA knows the difference and routes each one to the right channel automatically.

Step 04

Team notified

A clean structured notification lands in Slack or Teams with all the key data extracted and ready to act on. One click to claim.
